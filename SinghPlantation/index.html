Welcome to Singh Nursery

Singh Nursery & Plantation was established by Dr. Raghubir Singh, in the year 2018 and is a leading supplier of plants to various state governments and central government agencies NGOs, Horticulturists and nurseries.

With his vast experience over 34 years, we are producing, cultivating and marketing different kinds of plants all over the country and we also undertake projects of landscaping. We own thousands of varieties of plants in the plant kingdom. Our plant production and marketing house is situated at Lucknow.

Our organization is a unique essence of greenery, freshness, immorality and indeed a green revolution. We are very proud to introduce the reputed green enterprise to those who endeavor nature and since our establishment we are being a part of India's richest biodiversity. We boost our customers from all cities of India namely as Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Bihar, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Maharastra, Telangana & Others who are a prominent part of this biome.

Our organization is led by Dr. Raghubir Singh having a practical experience of over 40 years in the landscaping and horticulture. With his able assistance he is managing the group of Landscape Architects, Horticulturist and other staff.
