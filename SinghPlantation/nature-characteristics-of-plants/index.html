Nature & Characteristics of Plants

  • Home
  • Nature & Characteristics of Plants

A home having well planned greenery not only enhances aesthetic beauty of place but also helps in contributing towards the environment. With wide choice of plant species available for home gardening as well as outdoor garden, each ones requirement is different.

For ensuring proper growth and maintenance of the plants, one needs to have understanding of nature and characteristics of plants including:

  • Their growth habit/environment
  • Soil & nutrient needs

Under favourable conditions, plants do flourish in their characteristic shapes & sizes. Some of these conditions include:s

  • Most house plants thrive well in semi-shades with foliage attaining bright colors when exposed to morning sun for few hours
  • For successfully growing ornamental plants, one should ensure that porous & well drained substrate is used be it in ground or in pot as it promotes plant’s growth better than fertile but sticky soil
